About Me

Hello! I’m Raksha Mendon, an artist who loves exploring creativity through colors, textures, and emotions. My work is inspired by nature, calm moments, and the beauty found in everyday surroundings.

I primarily work with watercolor and acrylic mediums, focusing on expressive backgrounds, marine life, and floral compositions. Each piece reflects my personal journey and artistic growth.

Through this gallery, I hope to share my passion and connect with people who appreciate art that brings peace, curiosity, and warmth.
